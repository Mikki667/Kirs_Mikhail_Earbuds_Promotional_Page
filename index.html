About

Squeezit Earbuds are designed for people who want their tech to feel a little less boring and a lot more fun. Inspired by classic alien icons, each earbud blends a deep metallic purple shell with neon green accents and a friendly extraterrestrial face. The rounded shape sits comfortably in your ear, while the smooth surface makes them easy to grip, pop in, and go.

Behind the playful look, Squeezit delivers clear, immersive sound for music, games, and videos. The matching charging case keeps your earbuds powered and protected, with the same bold UFO-style design that stands out the moment you open it. Explore the earbuds in full 3D on this site, spin them in AR, and zoom in on every detail before they ever reach your ears.
